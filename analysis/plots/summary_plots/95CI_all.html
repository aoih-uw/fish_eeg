Individual Subject 95% Confidence Intervals by Stimulus Frequency and Amplitude

This visualization displays the 95% confidence intervals for auditory evoked potential responses from individual spotted ratfish (Hydrolagus colliei) subjects across different stimulus frequencies (55-880 Hz) and amplitudes.

Each panel represents a different frequency, with separate traces for each subject and test repetition. The shaded areas represent the 95% confidence intervals, with lines connecting the midpoints. The dashed horizontal line at zero provides a reference for determining statistical significance in the auditory response, allowing comparison of individual variability in responses across subjects.
