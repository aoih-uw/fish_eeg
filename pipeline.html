Analysis Pipeline

1. Data Loading

Trials parsed from MATLAB .mat structured as (frequency, amplitude) across four EEG channels.

2. Cleaning

Artifact rejection using RMS > mean + 3×SD. Channel trial counts balanced post-filter.

3. Bandpass Filter

70–1400 Hz passband removes irrelevant and low-frequency noise.

4. ICA Denoising

Components ranked by 2f SNR; weighted reconstruction preserves phase alignment.

5. Period Separation

Latency-adjusted segmentation into prestim (OFF) and stimresp (ON) windows.

6. Bootstrap & CI

100 resamples; 95% CI of (stimresp − prestim) SNR used for significance at 2f.
